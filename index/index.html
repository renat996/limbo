About the game

A journey through gloom,
where every step could be your last

No words. No explanations. Only darkness, silence, and the sound of your own footsteps. The world of Limbo does not forgive mistakes — it punishes them beautifully.

A boy with no name. A sister who may no longer exist. A path that leads deeper into shadow with every step.
