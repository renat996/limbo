Die and Retry

The gameplay of Limbo relies heavily on trial and error. The dark silhouette of the boy must navigate a hostile, monochromatic world where safety is an illusion.

Core Gameplay Elements

Trial and Death

The game is designed around inevitable failure. Every hidden pit, spike, or falling trap teaches you a deadly lesson. You will die often, but return instantly to try a new strategy.

Environmental Hazards

From giant silhouette spiders and sharp mechanical saws to brain-controlling glowing parasites that force you to walk in one direction — the entire world is built to stop you.

Physics Puzzles

Progress requires manipulating the environment. Dragging wooden crates, swinging on dangling ropes, timing jumps over rising tides, and shifting gravitational fields are key to survival.
